What We Are

VirtX is a multi-disciplinary team of researchers, educators, industry partners, and students loosely based around the University of Sydney who are passionate about XR.
Established at the University of Sydney Techlab in 2018, this team openly shares resources for bringing XR to the masses, through workshops, demonstrations, free content and openly published research.
